Frequently Asked Questions

What types of medical trips do you cover?

We transport patients to hospitals, dialysis centers, rehab facilities, specialist offices, imaging centers, pharmacies, and any other healthcare-related destinations.

Do you offer insurance or Medicaid rides?

Please contact us directly to discuss insurance coordination and available payment arrangements for qualifying patients.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend 24–48 hours advance notice. However, we do our best to accommodate same-day requests. Recurring schedules can be set up for maximum convenience.

Can a family member ride along?

Yes! Companions, caretakers, or family members are always welcome to accompany the patient at no extra charge (vehicle capacity permitting).
