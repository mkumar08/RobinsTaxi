Frequently Asked Questions

Which areas do your sightseeing tours cover?

We cover the Hudson Valley, Orange County, New York City, Long Island, and the greater Tri-State area. Custom routes to other destinations are available — just ask!

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 24–48 hours in advance to secure your preferred date and time. Same-day bookings may be available based on driver availability.

Can I bring luggage or a stroller?

Yes! We offer sedans, SUVs, and vans to accommodate your group size and any luggage, strollers, or equipment you need to bring along.

Do you offer group discounts?

Absolutely. Call us to discuss group pricing for parties of 4 or more — we'll find the best vehicle and rate for your group.
