ABOUT OUR COMPANY

Robin's Taxi was started up years back as a local transportation business offering complete taxi service in Middletown NY and surrounding regions. By offering professional, dependable service over the years, we have earned the trust of hundreds of satisfied customers.

You can book on-demand or schedule a ride in advance. We serve most of Orange County as well as airport transportation to LaGuardia, Newark, and JFK. We also service nearby stadiums, venues, and corporate accounts. Today, that compact business has grown into a full-service land transportation company serving not just Middletown but the entire Tri-State area with skilled, well-trained drivers and a modern fleet of Cars, SUVs and Vans.

At Robin's Taxi, we deeply appreciate the opportunity to serve our local community. Choose Robin's Taxi for a transportation experience that goes above and beyond. We are dedicated to getting you where you need to be, when you need to be there, with the utmost care and professionalism.
